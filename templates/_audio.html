Pricing

I am more interested in being a part of your creative process than a billable invoice, so let's sit down together and figure out what fits best for your ideas. For clarity, here is a good foothold to understand the rates I charge for audio work.

Tracking / Overdub Day rate (10 hours): $250
Hourly rate: $35/hr
Mixing: $175/song
Mastering: $75/song

Anything else: let's figure something out what works for you and if I'm a good fit.

You might have a whole project in mind and the idea might be a moving target -- I'm more interested in understanding what you are hoping to make. I'll even buy your coffee (!) if we sit down to work something out.
